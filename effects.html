Effects

Effects alter the audio in stereo tracks.

Note: Using some effects can elevate the peak levels of waveforms.
Table 1. Effects Index
Effect Function
Amplify Increases or decreases the volume of the audio
Normalize Sets the maximum amplitude of a track
Fade In Applies a linear fade-in to the audio
Fade Out Applies a linear fade-out to the audio
Repair Fixes one short click, pop, or glitch in the audio
Change Pitch Changes the pitch of a selection without changing its tempo
Change Speed Changes the speed of a selection also changing its pitch
Change Tempo Changes the tempo of a selection without changing its pitch
Vocal Reduction Removes center-panned audio from a stereo track
Vocorder Synthesizes audio in the left channel of a stereo track
