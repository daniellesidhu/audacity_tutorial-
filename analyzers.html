Analyzers

Analyzers perform analysis on stereo tracks.

Note: Analyzers do not change or create audio.
Table 1. Analyzers Index
Analyzer Function
Contrast Analyzes a stereo track to determine the average RMS
Measure RMS Analyzes a stereo track to measure the RMS levels
Beat Finder Analyzes a stereo track to find amplitude irregularities
Plot Spectrum Analyzes a stereo track and converts it into a frequency vs. amplitude graph
