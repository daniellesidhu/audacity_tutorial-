What is Audacity?

Audacity is a free multi-track recorder and editor that is compatible with Windows, macOS, and GNU/Linux. The Audacity software is developed and maintained by volunteers, and distributed under the GNU General Public License.

With Audacity, users can:
  • Record audio
  • Edit sound files
  • Record computer playback
  • Cut, copy, and mix sounds together
  • Change the tempo, speed, and pitch of recordings
  • Convert records and tapes into digital recordings
