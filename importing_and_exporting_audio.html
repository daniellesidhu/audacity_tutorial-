Importing and Exporting Audio

Learn how to import and export audio tracks.

Audacity can work with audio files in the following formats:
  • WAV
  • AIFF
  • MP3
  • Ogg Vorbis
  • FLAC
  • MP2
Note: Download the FFmpg library Audacity plug-in to export in more audio formats including AC3, AMR(NB), M4A(AAC), and WMA.
